## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add friends?
Tap the "Add Friends" button on the Home screen. You can share your unique invite code with friends, or enter their invite code to connect.

### How do I send a doodle?
1. Draw on the canvas on the Home screen
2. Tap the "Send" button
3. Select one or more friends
4. Tap "Send"

### How do I see doodles on my home screen?
1. Long-press on your iPhone home screen
2. Tap the "+" button in the top corner
3. Search for "Squibble"
4. Add the widget to your home screen

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account, doodles, and friend connections.

### How do I restore my premium subscription?
Go to Profile > Settings > Premium Status > Restore Purchases.

### I'm not receiving push notifications
1. Go to iPhone Settings > Squibble
2. Make sure Notifications are enabled
3. Check that you have a stable internet connection
